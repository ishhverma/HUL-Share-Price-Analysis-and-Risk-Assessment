# 📈 HUL Share Price Analysis & Risk Assessment

A comprehensive equity research project analyzing Hindustan Unilever Ltd. (HUL) using historical market data, descriptive statistics, risk analysis, and quantitative finance techniques.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the historical performance of HUL over the last five years to evaluate its growth, volatility, market sensitivity, and investment risk.

The report combines financial analysis with statistical techniques commonly used in Equity Research and Investment Analysis.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze HUL's historical stock performance
- Compare HUL with Nifty 50
- Study return distribution
- Measure market correlation
- Calculate Beta
- Estimate Value at Risk (VaR)
- Evaluate HUL as a long-term investment

## Key Findings

- HUL behaves as a defensive FMCG stock.
- Weak positive correlation with Nifty 50.
- Lower volatility than the overall market.
- Bottom-Up Beta provides a more reliable estimate than Regression Beta.
- Low Value at Risk indicates relatively stable downside risk.
